# Kentucky Cannabis Conference 2027 — Operations Plan

**Event Dates:** March 2027 (TBD specific dates)  
**Location:** Louisville, KY (venue TBD)  
**Owner:** Kentucky NORML  
**Status:** Planning Phase

---

## Executive Overview

The Kentucky Cannabis Conference 2027 is transitioning from a traditional multi-track conference to an integrated ecosystem gathering featuring relationship building, shared experiences, and the inaugural Consumer Choice Awards.

**Key Success Metrics:**
- 200+ attendees (vs. 145+ in 2024)
- High-quality partner participation (KCIA, regulators, legislators, industry)
- Awards program drives 30%+ of registration interest
- 90%+ sponsor satisfaction
- Post-event partnerships formed

### Wednesday — Conference Day 1

**7:30 AM – 9:00 AM** — Breakfast Conversations
- Hosted tables with themes:
  - Healthcare
  - Policy
  - Consumer Protection
  - First-Time Attendees
  - Industry Professionals
- Table hosts assigned (Kentucky NORML, KCIA, partners)
- ~250 people

**9:00 AM – 10:00 AM** — State of the Kentucky Cannabis Industry
- Owner: KCIA
- Main stage, 60 minutes
- Topics: market development, license progress, regulatory status, economic impact
- Expected audience: 200+

**10:00 AM – 11:00 AM** — Coffee & Conversations
- Speakeasy Hub
- Open networking time
- No formal programming

**11:00 AM – 12:00 PM** — State of the Kentucky Cannabis Consumer
- Owner: Kentucky NORML
- Main stage, 60 minutes
- Topics: patient access, consumer protection, legislative priorities, program challenges
- Expected audience: 150+

**12:00 PM – 1:30 PM** — Hosted Lunches
- "The lunch is the session"
- Multiple host tables (KCIA, Kentucky NORML, industry partners)
- Attendees choose table by topic/interest
- Networking during meal

**1:30 PM – 3:00 PM** — Open Gathering Time
- Meetings, conversations, coffee
- No formal programming
- Speakeasy Hub, exec boardrooms for meetings

**3:00 PM – 4:00 PM** — Office of Medical Cannabis Program Update
- Owner: OMC (Office of Medical Cannabis)
- Main stage, 60 minutes
- Program updates, regulatory information
- Expected audience: 100-150

**4:00 PM – 6:00 PM** — Free Time
- Reset, meet people, take meetings
- No formal programming
- Speakeasy Hub available

**Evening** — Unofficial Gatherings
- Hotel bar, restaurants, sponsor dinners
- No official programming

**Owner Responsibilities:**
- Session logistics (speaker tech, timing, moderation)
- Breakfast table coordination & hosts
- Lunch host coordination
- Speakeasy Hub supervision
- Catering coordination

## Risk Management & Contingencies

### High-Risk Areas

**Speaker No-Shows**
- Mitigation: Backup speakers identified for keynotes
- Mitigation: Recorded remarks as backup
- Contingency: Kentucky NORML exec can fill 30-min session if needed

**Low Attendance**
- Mitigation: Early bird pricing to drive registrations (Sept-Dec 2026)
- Mitigation: Partnership marketing with KCIA, dispensaries, patient groups
- Contingency: Adjust session count to match attendance

**Venue Issues**
- Mitigation: Site visit in August, monthly check-ins
- Mitigation: Backup venue identified by October 2026
- Contingency: Virtual backup plan for critical sessions

**Technology Failures**
- Mitigation: Full tech rehearsal in February
- Mitigation: Backup WiFi/hotspot systems
- Contingency: Low-tech backup (flip charts, printed materials)

**Awards Platform Issues**
- Mitigation: Testing in December 2026 and January 2027
- Mitigation: Backup manual voting system
- Contingency: Extend voting by 1 week if platform fails
