## If You're Behind on This Timeline

**What to deprioritize (NOT critical for launch):**
- Perfect marketing materials (good is good enough)
- 100% sponsor commitments (aim for 3-5, rest comes in Oct-Nov)
- All speaker confirmations (get 50%, rest confirms in Oct-Dec)

**What NOT to deprioritize:**
- Venue contract (must be signed by early August)
- Awards platform (takes longest, must start immediately)
- Team clarity on roles (prevents chaos later)

**If seriously behind on major items:**
- Extend 90-day plan to 120 days? (Real, not ideal, but possible)
- Scale down event size? (Venue for 150 instead of 250?)
- Delay awards program? (Not recommended—it's core differentiation)
