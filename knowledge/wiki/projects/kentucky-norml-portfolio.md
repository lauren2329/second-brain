## 1. Kentucky Cannabis Conference

**Status:** Active Development

This is currently the organization's largest annual event.

### Current Direction

**Moving away from:**
- Traditional trade show model
- Endless panels
- Room-hopping conferences

**Moving toward:**
- Community gathering
- Shared experiences
- Networking
- Relationship building
- Industry collaboration

### Current Structure Under Discussion

| | |
|---|---|
| Tuesday | Arrival |
| Wednesday | Conference Day 1 |
| Thursday | Conference Day 2 + Awards Program |
| Friday | Departure |

### Potential Programming Ownership

- Consumer Rights Update

- State of the Industry Report

## Organizational Evolution

Looking at everything discussed over the last several months, Kentucky NORML's operational focus is increasingly shifting from being primarily an advocacy group to becoming a **statewide cannabis ecosystem convener**—bringing together consumers, patients, industry, policymakers, and community partners while maintaining its consumer-protection mission.
