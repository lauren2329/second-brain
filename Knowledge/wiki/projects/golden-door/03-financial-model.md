- Arrow restricted credit must not be counted toward payroll, rent, debt service, or general liquidity.

7. Cash-flow waterfall.
8. Break-even analysis.
9. Scenario analysis: conservative, moderate, strong.
10. Actual-versus-budget dashboard.

## Cash Waterfall
1. Gross gaming receipts recorded by CGN.
2. Required state and vendor obligations reserved.
3. Operating expenses funded.
4. Minimum cash reserve restored.
5. Debt service paid according to written agreements.
6. Approved VMP management fees paid under contract.
7. Remaining funds retained for charitable purposes, capital needs, and board-approved expansion.

## Reserve Policy Draft
Opening decisions should preserve enough unrestricted cash for:
- Three months of fixed occupancy costs.
- One full month of staffing and contractor costs.
- Insurance deductibles and emergency repairs.
- Regulatory or licensing delays.
- At least one missed or delayed vendor payment cycle.

## Controls
- Separate bank accounts and bookkeeping by entity.
- No personal spending from CGN accounts.
- Weekly cash report during launch.
- Monthly board-level financial package.
- Two-person approval for large or related-party payments.
- Written documentation for loans, advances, reimbursements, and management fees.
