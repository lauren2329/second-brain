# Golden Door Staffing

## Objective
Maintain compliant floor coverage, dependable opening and closing coverage, and a staffing model that can scale without placing all operational responsibility on Lauren and Matthew.

### Attendants and Support

## Coverage Principles
- At least one qualified chairperson must be onsite whenever required by law or license conditions.
- Opening coverage begins one hour before public hours.
- Closing coverage continues one hour after public hours.
- No one works alone during cash close, incidents, or high-volume periods.
- Staffing assignments must distinguish charitable gaming duties from VMP management work.

## Proposed Public Hours
- Monday: closed
- Tuesday-Thursday: 11:00 a.m.-7:00 p.m.
- Friday-Saturday: 11:00 a.m.-8:00 p.m.
- Sunday: 1:00 p.m.-5:00 p.m.

## Shift Design
