# Golden Door Compliance Framework

## Purpose
Keep charitable gaming, nonprofit governance, money handling, staffing, vendor relationships, and VMP management services legally and operationally separated.

## Core Rules
- CGN operates the charitable gaming activity.
- Gaming funds belong to CGN.
- VMP may be paid only under written, board-approved agreements where legally permitted.
- Related-party transactions must be disclosed, documented, and approved by disinterested decision
