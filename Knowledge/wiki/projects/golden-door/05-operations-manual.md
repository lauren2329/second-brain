# Golden Door Operations Manual

## Operating Objective
Run a consistent, safe, welcoming, and compliant charitable gaming lounge with clear daily controls and minimal dependence on any one person.

## Core Systems
1. Opening and closing
2. Cash handling and reconciliation
3. Machine floor operations
4. Customer service
5. Incident response
6. Cleaning and facility care
7. Vendor coordination
8. Recordkeeping
9. Technology and access control
10. Management reporting

## Daily Opening Standard
- Assigned chairperson or authorized responsible person on site before public opening.
- Facility walk-through completed.
- Restrooms, exits, ADA routes, and customer areas checked.
- Machines and network status verified.
- Cash till counted by two people where required by policy.
- Opening balance documented.
- Security systems checked.
- Required notices and licenses visibly posted.
- Shift log opened.

## During Operating Hours
- Maintain required on-site supervision.
- Record machine, payment, customer, safety, and security incidents immediately.
- Keep restricted areas controlled.
- Do not mix gaming funds with retail, personal, or VMP funds.
- Escalate disputes instead of improvising policy.
- Track maintenance issues until closed.

## Daily Closing Standard
- Stop transactions at the posted closing time.
- Reconcile cash and system totals.
- Document discrepancies before staff leave.
- Secure cash, keys, records, and devices.
- Complete cleaning and safety walk-through.
- Confirm doors, alarms, thermostats, and equipment status.
- Submit shift report.

## Required Logs
- Opening and closing checklist
- Cash reconciliation
- Incident log
- Machine downtime and maintenance
- Visitor/vendor access
- Cleaning and facilities issues
- Staff and chairperson coverage
- Complaints and resolutions

## Management Rhythm
- Daily: closing report and cash summary
- Weekly: operations, staffing, machine performance, incidents, and cash review
- Monthly: board financial package, compliance review, vendor review, and KPI report

## Document Control
SOPs created for this manual must identify an owner, approval date, review date, version, and related forms.
