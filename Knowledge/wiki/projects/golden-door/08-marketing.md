# Golden Door Marketing System

## Purpose
Build steady local awareness, convert that attention into repeat visits, and maintain a consistent public image without depending on one-time launch hype.

## Positioning
Golden Door should feel like an upscale social lounge with charitable purpose, not a generic gaming hall. The customer experience should emphasize comfort, hospitality, cleanliness, safety, and community.

## Core Audiences
- Adults seeking a comfortable social gaming experience.
- Local women who prefer a polished, welcoming environment.
- Community members interested in charitable impact.
- Local partners, vendors, and civic organizations.
- Referral sources that serve older adults and socially isolated adults.

## Core Messages
1. Comfortable, polished, and welcoming.
2. Social gaming with charitable purpose.
3. Safe, clean, and professionally operated.
4. Local money supporting local community benefit.
5. A place people can return to regularly.

## Marketing Channels
- Facebook and Instagram.
- Google Business Profile.
- Email and SMS list.
- Local community groups.
- Direct partnerships and referral relationships.
- Printed cards, flyers, and in-store signage.
- Local media and opening-week outreach.

## Launch Campaign
### Pre-Opening
- Establish brand standards and approved language.
- Publish opening-soon content.
- Build a waitlist and founding-customer email list.
- Recruit referral partners.
- Prepare photography, signage, FAQs, and launch offers.

### Opening Week
- Soft opening before grand opening.
- Stagger invitations so operations can be tested.
- Capture testimonials and customer feedback.
- Track source of every new customer.
- Publish daily updates without overstating demand or results.

### First 90 Days
- Focus on repeat visits, not only new traffic.
- Introduce recurring weekly themes.
- Build a customer referral program within legal limits.
- Test small campaigns and record cost per visit.
- Remove channels that do not create measurable traffic.

## Recurring Content Categories
- Customer experience and lounge atmosphere.
- Charitable impact and community purpose.
- Staff and volunteer introductions.
- Hours, events, and weekly themes.
- Responsible-play reminders.
- Behind-the-scenes preparation.
- Community partner spotlights.

## Required Assets
- Brand guide.
- Approved logo files.
- Photo library.
- One-page project description.
- Frequently asked questions.
- Social media templates.
- Printed referral card.
- Media release template.
- Monthly content calendar.

## Metrics
- New customers by source.
- Repeat-visit rate.
- Email and SMS list growth.
- Cost per acquired customer.
- Average customer value.
- Referral partner activity.
- Reviews and customer satisfaction.
- Campaign revenue compared with cost.

## Controls
- No claims that are misleading, unverifiable, or inconsistent with gaming rules.
- No commingling of CGN and VMP branding where entity roles must remain clear.
- Promotions must receive compliance review before publication.
- Customer data must be stored in approved systems and used with consent.
- Marketing expenses must be documented and tied to the correct entity.

## Immediate Build List
- Finalize customer profile.
- Write approved public description.
- Create 90-day launch calendar.
- Establish partnership target list.
- Build customer-source tracking.
- Create review-request and referral scripts.
