# Custom FlyLady Cleaning Zones for 600 Kirtley Way

## House Overview
- **Bedrooms:** 1 downstairs, 2 upstairs
- **Bathrooms:** 2 full bathrooms
- **Main Living Space:** Open-concept living/dining/kitchen (one long area)
- **Garage:** 10' × 22' (split: half playroom, half storage)
- **Special Features:** Back patio in daily use
- **Layout Advantage:** Open-concept makes weekly cleaning flow naturally through connected zones

---

## Zone Breakdown & Rotation

### ZONE 1: Entryway & Front Door (Monday)
**Focus:** Entry point and first impression

**Includes:**
- Front entryway/foyer
- Entry closet
- Front door area

**Weekly Tasks:**
- Vacuum/sweep entry
- Dust entry table/surfaces
- Organize entry closet
- Wipe down door and light switches
- Check for clutter by entry
- Clean entry mirrors/windows

---

### ZONE 2: Open Living/Dining/Kitchen & Back Patio (Tuesday)
**Focus:** Main living space—the heart of the home, includes daily patio use

**Includes:**
- Living room
- Dining area
- Kitchen (all appliances, counters, cabinets)
- Back patio (daily use area)

**Weekly Tasks:**
- Deep clean kitchen: appliances (inside microwave, oven, fridge)
- Clean all kitchen counters and backsplash
- Wipe down kitchen cabinets (rotate which ones)
- Clean dining table and chairs
- Vacuum/sweep entire open living area
- Mop kitchen/dining tile
- Hose down/sweep back patio
- Wipe light switches and baseboards throughout
- Organize kitchen junk drawer/utensil drawers

---

### ZONE 3: Downstairs Bedroom & Bathroom (Wednesday)
**Focus:** Private guest/downstairs sleeping area and adjoining bathroom

**Includes:**
- Downstairs bedroom
- Associated full bathroom (or primary bathroom if downstairs)

**Weekly Tasks:**
- Change bed linens
- Dust all surfaces and baseboards
- Vacuum/sweep bedroom
- Clean bathroom: toilet, shower/tub, sink, mirrors
- Mop bathroom floor
- Organize bathroom drawers/cabinets
- Wipe down light switches

---

### ZONE 4: Upstairs Bedrooms & Hallway (Thursday)
**Focus:** Sleeping and bathing areas upstairs

**Includes:**
- Bedroom 1 (upstairs)
- Bedroom 2 (upstairs)
- Upstairs hallway
- Upstairs full bathroom
- Linen closet (if located here)

**Weekly Tasks:**
- Change bed linens (both beds)
- Dust all surfaces
- Vacuum/sweep both bedrooms and hallway
- Clean upstairs bathroom: toilet, shower/tub, sink, mirrors
- Mop bathroom floor
- Organize hallway closet/linen closet
- Wipe baseboards and light switches

---

### ZONE 5: Playroom, Storage & Garage (Friday)
**Focus:** Play and storage spaces; end-of-week reset

**Includes:**
- Garage playroom area (5' × 11')
- Garage storage area (5' × 11')
- Laundry area (if applicable)
- Mudroom/transition spaces

**Weekly Tasks:**
- Toy organization and rotation in playroom
- Sweep entire garage
- Organize/consolidate storage area
- Wipe down shelves (rotate sections)
- Check for items to donate/discard
- Organize laundry supplies
- Clean out washer/dryer lint traps

---

## Saturday & Sunday: Catch-up & Family Days
- **Saturday:** Weekly detailed cleaning - anything that needs extra attention
- **Sunday:** Meal prep, planning, light tidying (family time focus)

## Daily Non-Negotiables (Every Day)
1. Make beds in all bedrooms
2. Wash dishes/load dishwasher immediately
3. One load of laundry (wash, dry, fold, put away)
4. 15-minute morning and evening kitchen reset
5. Tidy playroom toys before bed

---

## Special Considerations for Your Home
- **Back Patio:** Check weather daily; prioritize in summer months during Zone 2
- **Playroom:** Plan toy rotation and deep clean during Zone 5
- **Storage:** Use Zone 5 to audit and maintain organization system
- **Laundry:** With 5 people (assumption), prioritize daily laundering
