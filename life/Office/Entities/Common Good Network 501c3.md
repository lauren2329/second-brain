# Common Good Network (CGN) – Identity Framework

## 1. Core Identity

**Legal Status:**  
501(c)(3) Educational Nonprofit  

**Leadership:**  
Women-led board  

**Domain Focus:**  
Wellness  

**Subtopics:**
- Physical Health  
- Mental Health  

**Method:**  
All activities are delivered through education, public information, and community-based learning.

**Audience:**  
Open to the general public.

---

## 2. Positioning Statement

**Primary Version:**  
Common Good Network is a 501(c)(3) educational nonprofit focused on wellness. We provide accessible education on physical and mental health through community-based programming and public information.

**Grant-Ready Version:**  
Common Good Network is a 501(c)(3) educational nonprofit dedicated to improving access to wellness education. Through community-based programming and public information, we deliver practical education on physical and mental health to the general public.

---

## 3. Program Structure

### Wellness (Primary Domain)

All programs fall under wellness and are delivered through education.

#### A. Physical Health
- Chronic illness education  
- Preventative care education  
- Body care, nutrition, and daily functioning  

#### B. Mental Health
- Emotional regulation  
- Stress education  
- Neurodivergence (ADHD, autism, etc.)  
- Behavioral patterns and habit formation  

---

## 4. Delivery Methods

- Workshops and classes  
- Conferences and public events  
- Digital guides and content  
- Educational resource navigation  

---

## 5. Compliance Guardrails

**Allowed:**
- Educational programming  
- Public information and resource guides  
- Community education events  

**Not Allowed under CGN:**
- Direct services or case management  
- Clinical treatment or diagnosis  
- Lobbying or advocacy (handled by Kentucky NORML)

---

## 6. Ecosystem Alignment

- CGN → Wellness education (nonprofit)  
- Kentucky NORML → Advocacy and policy (c4)  
- Wonderland Wellness → Private sector wellness ecosystem  
- Verdant Ventures → Operations and management  

**Cannabis Positioning:**  
Included strictly as a wellness education topic (public health and patient education context only).

---
