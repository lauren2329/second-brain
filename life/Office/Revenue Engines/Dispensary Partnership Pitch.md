# Dispensary Partnership with KYNORML

## The Ask: Why Partner With KYNORML?

KYNORML reaches engaged wellness-focused cannabis users and advocates. We provide:
- **Credibility** — Association with education-driven organization
- **Customer Access** — Direct reach to 2,000+ members
- **Brand Visibility** — Featured in email, social, events, directory
- **Community Trust** — Positioned as trusted partner (not generic ad)

---

## Partnership Options

### Option 1: Directory Listing (Ongoing)
**Investment:** $100-250/month  
**You Get:**
- Featured listing in KYNORML Member Directory (searchable by location, product type, etc.)
- Logo and description
- Link to your website
- Member referrals from directory

**Best For:** Dispensaries wanting consistent customer discovery

---

### Option 2: Monthly Sponsor (Email + Social)
**Investment:** $500/month  
**You Get:**
- Featured in KYNORML monthly newsletter (5,000+ subscribers)
- 2-3 social media posts per month (reaching 10,000+ followers)
- Logo/link placement on website
- "Presented by [Dispensary]" branding on one monthly event

**Best For:** Dispensaries wanting consistent brand exposure

---

### Option 3: Event Sponsorship (One-Time)
**Investment:** $1,000-5,000 (per event)  
**Event Examples:**
- Monthly community gathering/workshop
- Quarterly networking event
- Annual conference (larger investment)

**You Get:**
- Logo/booth space at event
- Speaking/tasting opportunity
- Email promotion to members pre-event
- Social media promotion
- Member list (attendees)

**Best For:** Launching a new product, seasonal promotion, or local market entry

---

### Option 4: Workshop Sponsor (Hands-On)
**Investment:** $2,000-3,000  
**You Host/Run:**
- KYNORML member workshop at your dispensary (education-focused, not sales-focused)
- Example: "Wellness + Cannabis: Product Selection for [Condition]"
- Served by your team, KYNORML provides marketing/attendee recruitment

**You Get:**
- 30-50 engaged members attending your location
- Foot traffic/conversion
- Member relationship building
- Logo/credit from KYNORML

**Best For:** Dispensaries wanting direct customer relationship building

---

### Option 5: Annual Partnership Tier
**Investment:** $5,000-10,000/year  
**You Get (Everything):**
- Directory listing (premium, featured)
- Monthly newsletter sponsorship
- 4 social sponsorships per year
- Logo on website/materials
- One workshop or event sponsorship
- Quarterly business review with KYNORML leadership
- Access to member feedback/insights

**Best For:** Dispensaries wanting sustained, deep partnership

---

## Member Value (Why They Listen)

KYNORML members join because they want:
- Credible health information
- Community and advocacy power
- Access to vetted, trusted businesses

When KYNORML sponsors are featured, members view them as **vetted partners**, not generic ads.

---

## How It Works

1. **You decide on partnership level** (Option 1-5)
2. **Sign sponsorship agreement** (one-time or ongoing)
3. **We handle promotion** — Email, social, directory, events
4. **You get customer access and brand credibility**
5. **We track results** — Email opens, event attendance, website clicks

---

## What This Is NOT

- We don't sell your products directly
- We don't add you to email list without member consent
- We don't promise sales or customer numbers (we track reach, not conversion)
- We don't dilute your brand (one sponsor per category at a time)

---

## What This IS

- Credibility by association
- Access to engaged community
- Brand visibility among people who care about wellness + cannabis
- Long-term customer relationship opportunity
