# KYNORML Consistency Engine: Automation + VMP

## Strategy
- Automate everything that repeats (scheduling, posting, reminders)
- VMP handles: content creation, member/sponsor communication, decision-making
- Reduce decision fatigue and activation energy

---

## Revenue Engines: Automation vs. Human Work

### SOCIAL MEDIA
**Automate:**
- Scheduling posts (Buffer, Later, Meta Business Suite)
- Cross-posting same content across platforms
- Recurring reminder to batch-create content
- Auto-responses for common DMs

**VMP Owns:**
- Batch content creation (4 weeks at a time)
- Community engagement (respond to comments)
- Crisis/urgent posts (real-time)

**Minimum Viable Consistency:** 3 posts/week (scheduled in advance)

---

### EMAIL
**Automate:**
- Scheduled send times (Mailchimp, ConvertKit)
- Welcome sequence for new members
- Monthly newsletter template
- Sponsorship opportunity blasts (template)

**VMP Owns:**
- Write newsletter content (batch monthly)
- Member updates (urgent)
- Sponsor communications

**Minimum Viable Consistency:** 1 email/week

---

### BLOG
**Automate:**
- Auto-post blog to social media + email
- SEO scheduling (Yoast)
- Internal linking automation

**VMP Owns:**
- Write posts (2-4/month, batch)
- Schedule publication dates

**Minimum Viable Consistency:** 2 posts/month

---

## VMP Workflow (Sustainable with ADHD/Executive Dysfunction)

### Weekly (2-3 hrs)
- Respond to comments/DMs (batched)
- Check/respond to emails
- Handle sponsor/member requests

### Bi-Weekly (2 hrs)
- Batch create social content for next 2 weeks (8 posts)
- Record/edit any video content

### Monthly (3-4 hrs)
- Write/schedule newsletter (2000 words, once)
- Write/publish 1-2 blog posts
- Review analytics (what worked?)

### Quarterly (4-6 hrs)
- Sponsor prospecting calls
- Member retention outreach
- Conference/event planning reviews

---

## What NOT to Automate

- Actual content creation (writing, recording, design)
- Sales/relationship building
- Decision-making on strategy
- Member/sponsor-specific requests
- Community moderation (someone has to read comments)

---

## Consistency Safeguards (Anti-Burnout)

**Pre-created content buffer:**
- Always have 2-4 weeks of social media scheduled in advance
- Newsletter template ready to fill-in monthly
- Blog post drafts queued

**Automation handles the rest:**
- Posting happens on schedule (zero human activation)
- Reminders trigger actions (not relying on memory)
- Renewals/confirmations auto-send

**Result:** Even if VMP disappears for a week, the engines keep running

---

## Success Metrics

- Social: 3 posts/week, 100% scheduled
- Email: 1 email/week, 100% scheduled
- Blog: 2 posts/month, auto-synced to social + email
- Memberships: 0 lapsed renewals due to communication
- Sponsors: All receive promised communications on time

---

## Implementation Order

1. Set up social scheduling (Buffer) + batch content workflow
2. Set up email automation (Mailchimp) + monthly template
3. Set up member portal (Mighty Networks or similar)
4. Set up directory self-service
5. Ecommerce (Shopify + Printful)
6. Conference automation (Eventbrite)

Start with #1 and #2. Those are your revenue leverage points.
