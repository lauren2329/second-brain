# Sales Playbook: Business Memberships & Sponsorships

## Sales Person's Job

Sell business memberships ($500-2,500/year) and sponsorships ($500-10,000/year) to Kentucky cannabis businesses and aligned companies.

---

## Revenue Target

**Monthly target:** $3,000-5,000 in new business membership + sponsorship revenue

**Example combinations to hit target:**
- 3 business members @ $500/yr = $1,500 + 1 gold sponsor @ $5,000 = $6,500/month
- 5 business members @ $1,200 avg + 1 silver sponsor @ $2,500 = $8,500/month
- 2 partner members @ $2,500 + 1 bronze sponsor @ $1,000 = $6,000/month

---

## Weekly Workflow

### Monday (Prospecting - 2-3 hours)
**Task:** Identify + outreach to 8-12 prospects (dispensaries, cultivators, ancillary businesses, service providers)

**Process:**
1. Source list:
   - Google Maps: "cannabis dispensary Kentucky"
   - Weedmaps: Browse by location
   - LinkedIn: Search "cannabis" + "Kentucky"
   - Local business directories
   
2. Qualify (quick check):
   - Is this a Kentucky cannabis business?
   - Can I find owner/manager contact?
   - Are they likely to have budget for partnerships?

3. Send email:
   - Subject: "[Business Name] + KY NORML Partnership"
   - 3 sentences max
   - Include link to partnership doc (business membership or sponsorship pitch)
   - CTA: "15-min call this week?"

4. Log in pipeline: Name, contact, date, business type, interest level

---

### Tuesday (Follow-Up Calls - 2-3 hours)
**Task:** Call warm leads from Monday + previous week

**Process:**
1. Check email for responses
2. Call anyone who opened/responded or said they'd review
3. Call script:

   **Opening:**
   "Thanks for your time. Quick question: Are you looking to increase visibility with Kentucky cannabis consumers right now?"
   
   **Listen. If yes:**
   "We offer two main options: business membership (ongoing promotion + directory listing) or sponsorship (larger visibility + program support). Which sounds more relevant?"
   
   **If interested in business membership:**
   "We have three tiers: Business Member ($500), Supporting ($1,000), or Partner ($2,500). What's your budget range?"
   
   **If interested in sponsorship:**
   "Sponsorships range from $500-10,000/year depending on visibility level. What's your budget and what kind of exposure are you looking for?"
   
   **Next:**
   "Let me send you the details + member testimonials. Can we jump on a call later this week to go over specifics?"

4. Log outcome: Interested / Not interested / Follow up in [X weeks]

---

### Wednesday (Consultations - 2-3 hours)
**Task:** Demo calls with interested prospects

**Process:**
1. Screen share: Partnership overview (business membership tiers or sponsorship levels)
2. Ask discovery questions:
   - How do you currently acquire customers?
   - What's your biggest challenge in the Kentucky market?
   - Are you looking for ongoing visibility or event-specific?
3. Match tier to their need:
   - Budget-conscious + new → Business Member ($500)
   - Established + want consistent → Supporting ($1,000)
   - Growth-focused + year-round → Partner ($2,500)
   - Event-specific → Sponsorship tier matching budget
4. Send proposal/agreement

---

### Thursday (Close & Onboard - 1-2 hours)
**Task:** Process deals

**Process:**
1. Receive signed agreement
2. Process payment (invoice, card, ACH)
3. Send welcome email with next steps:
   - What benefit are they getting this month?
   - When will first promotion happen?
   - How to update directory listing (if applicable)
4. Log in tracking: Closed date, benefit schedule, renewal date

---

### Friday (Pipeline Review - 1 hour)
**Task:** Review week + forecast

**Process:**
1. Count: How many contacted? Interested? Closed?
2. Calculate: On track for $3-5k/month?
3. Plan: If behind, add extra prospecting next week
4. Update pipeline spreadsheet

---

## Pipeline Tracking

**Spreadsheet columns:**
- Business Name
- Owner/Manager
- Contact Info
- Business Type (Dispensary / Cultivator / Ancillary / Service Provider / Aligned Brand)
- Date First Contacted
- Interest (Business Member / Sponsorship / Both / None)
- Budget Level (if known)
- Status (Prospect / Warm Lead / Demo Scheduled / Proposal Sent / Closed / Follow Up [Date])
- Deal Type & Value (if closed)
- Close Date
- Next Renewal Date
- Notes

---

## What Closes Deals

1. **Clarity** — They know exactly what they're paying for and what they get
2. **Speed** — Close in 1-2 weeks, not months
3. **Credibility** — Testimonials, case studies, member stories
4. **Specificity** — "You'll be featured in our December newsletter to 5,000+ members" is better than "promotional support"

---

## What Kills Deals

1. **Vague promises** — "We'll promote you" (no specifics)
2. **Slow follow-up** — They ask a question, you reply in 5 days
3. **Custom tiers** — They want something outside the menu (stick to the tiers)
4. **No proof** — They ask for examples, you have none

## Talking Points

**For Business Members:**
- "KY NORML connects you with consumers and patients actively looking for trusted businesses"
- "You're not just in a directory—we actively promote you through email, social, and referrals"
- "Our members choose businesses they trust. KY NORML endorsement matters"
- "From new startups to established dispensaries, businesses use KYNORML to build credibility"

**For Sponsors:**
- "You're not buying an ad. You're funding programs that benefit your customers"
- "Sponsorship associates your brand with Kentucky's leading cannabis education and advocacy organization"
- "Sponsors are recognized as partners committed to the Kentucky cannabis community"
- "From conference visibility to year-round placement, you choose your level of engagement"

## Sales Targets

**Per month:**
- Contacts made: 40-50
- Warm leads (interested): 10-15
- Demos: 5-8
- Closed: 2-4 deals
- Revenue: $3,000-5,000

**Per year:**
- Closed deals: 24-48
- Revenue: $36,000-60,000

---

## Messaging (Stay Consistent)

**Use:**
- "Business membership" (not "business plan" or "listing package")
- "Visibility, referrals, and community engagement"
- "Promotion through email, social, directory, and events"
- "Directory listing" (not "business profile" or "page")

**Don't use:**
- "Ads" or "advertising"
- "Customers" (use "members" or "consumers")
- "Traffic" (use "visibility" or "connections")

---

## Red Flags (Move On)

- They only want "sales leads" (not what KY NORML provides)
- They want custom tiers below minimum ($500)
- They ghost for 2+ weeks after "interested"
- They ask for promises KY NORML can't deliver

---

## Success

- Hitting $3-5k revenue target monthly
- 2-4 closed deals per month
- Pipeline has 10-15 warm leads at all times
- Referrals from happy members (best lead source)
- High renewal rate (happy businesses renew)
