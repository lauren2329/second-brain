# KYNORML Revenue Model Map

KYNORML educates consumers, advocates for patients, connects businesses with consumers, and convenes Kentucky's cannabis community.

---

## The 5 Revenue Programs

```
                    KYNORML
                        │
        ┌───────────────┼───────────────┐
        │               │               │
    MEMBERS         BUSINESSES      SPONSORS
        │               │               │
        ↓               ↓               ↓
   FUNDING          VISIBILITY        FUNDING
   ADVOCACY         + REFERRALS       + VISIBILITY
   EDUCATION        + CREDIBILITY
        │               │               │
        └───────────────┼───────────────┘
                        │
                    CONFERENCE
                        │
                    DONATIONS
```

### 1. [[KYNORML Revenue Engines|Individual Memberships]]
**What:** Supporter ($50), Advocate ($100), Champion ($250)/year  
**Members fund:** Advocacy, education, legislative action  
**They get:** Newsletter, alerts, community, conference

**Deliverable:** [[Operations - Simplified#1. MEMBERSHIPS (Monthly Newsletter + Action Alerts)|Monthly newsletter + action alerts]]

---

### 2. [[KYNORML Revenue Engines|Business Memberships]]
**What:** Business ($500), Supporting ($1,000), Partner ($2,500)/year  
**Businesses pay for:** Consumer visibility, referrals, credibility  
**They get:** Directory listing, promotion, spotlight, conference access

**Deliverable:** [[Operations - Simplified#2. BUSINESS MEMBER PROMOTION (Directory + Visibility)|Business promotion (directory, newsletter, social, referrals)]]

---

### 3. [[KYNORML Revenue Engines|Sponsorships]]
**What:** Community ($500) to Platinum ($10,000)/year  
**Sponsors fund:** KY NORML programs  
**They get:** Visibility, conference recognition, association with cause

**Deliverable:** [[Operations - Simplified#4. BUSINESS MEMBER FULFILLMENT (What You Promise)|Sponsor recognition and visibility]]

---

### 4. [[KYNORML Revenue Engines|Conference]]
**What:** Annual event, attendee tickets ($99-199), sponsor packages ($1k-10k+)  
**Attendees:** Education, networking, community  
**Sponsors:** Booth, visibility, engagement

**Deliverable:** [[Operations - Simplified#3. CONFERENCE (Annual Event)|Annual summit production + execution]]

---

### 5. [[KYNORML Revenue Engines|Donations]]
**What:** One-time or monthly giving  
**Donors fund:** Public programs (education, advocacy, access)  
**Outcome:** Programs that don't generate revenue

## How It Works: The Flywheel

```
INDIVIDUAL MEMBERS
    ↓
(Fund advocacy + education)
    ↓
KYNORML builds credibility with consumers
    ↓
BUSINESSES see engaged audience
    ↓
BUSINESSES join (pay for visibility)
    ↓
KYNORML promotes businesses to members
    ↓
CONSUMERS find trusted businesses
    ↓
BUSINESSES get value, renew membership
    ↓
SPONSORS see healthy community
    ↓
SPONSORS fund conference + programs
    ↓
CONFERENCE brings community together
    ↓
MEMBERS renew (stronger community)
    ↓
Cycle continues
```

## Operations: What Needs to Be Consistent

Only 4 things (everything else is secondary):

1. **[[Operations - Simplified#1. MEMBERSHIPS (Monthly Newsletter + Action Alerts)|Monthly Newsletter]]** — Members expect this. Sporadic = churn.

2. **[[Operations - Simplified#2. BUSINESS MEMBER PROMOTION (Directory + Visibility)|Business Member Promotion]]** — Businesses paid for visibility. Consistent delivery = retention.

3. **[[Operations - Simplified#3. CONFERENCE (Annual Event)|Annual Conference]]** — Major revenue driver + major member benefit.

4. **[[Operations - Simplified#4. BUSINESS MEMBER FULFILLMENT (What You Promise)|Fulfillment Tracking]]** — What you promised, you deliver. Track and execute.

**Everything else** (social media, blog, workshops, events) supports these 4 things or happens when you have capacity.

## Sales: Generate Revenue

[[Sales Playbook - Simplified|Sales person sells]] business memberships + sponsorships:

- **Prospect:** Email outreach to 8-12 Kentucky cannabis businesses/week
- **Demo:** 15-min call explaining tiers + value
- **Close:** Sign agreement, process payment
- **Target:** $3-5k/month (2-4 deals)

**Customer segments:**
- Dispensaries
- Cultivators
- Ancillary businesses
- Service providers
- Aligned brands

## What This Means

With 2 people + executive dysfunction:
- ✓ You can sustain 5 revenue programs
- ✓ You can automate delivery (scheduling, templates, reminders)
- ✓ You can batch work (newsletters 3x/year, spotlights quarterly, social monthly)
- ✓ You can scale sales (simple tiers, clear pitch, repeatable process)
- ✗ You cannot do everything perfectly
- ✗ You cannot be responsive + consistent at the same time (pick consistent)

**The key:** Do these 4 things consistently. Let everything else be bonus.
