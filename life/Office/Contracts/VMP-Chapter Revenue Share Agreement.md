# MANAGEMENT AND REVENUE SHARE AGREEMENT

**This Agreement** is entered into as of **[DATE]** between:

**Verdant Management Partners** ("VMP"), a [state] [LLC/company type], and

**[CHAPTER NAME], INC** ("Chapter"), a [state] nonprofit [corporation/organization].

Collectively referred to as the "Parties."

---

## 1. Services

VMP agrees to provide the following services to the Chapter at no upfront cost:

**1.1 Initial Setup (one-time)**
- Technology stack configuration: Google Workspace, Action Network, Zeffy, BillTrack50, Monday.com, Buffer/Meta Business Suite, Canva
- Brand setup: Canva workspace, templates, social media accounts
- Revenue stream activation: membership tiers, donation page, directory listing upgrade offer, merch shop
- Email system: welcome sequence, first campaign templates, segmentation
- Playbook customization: all NORML Chapter Box documents customized to Chapter's name, state, and brand

**1.2 Ongoing Management**
- Monthly email campaigns (minimum 2 per month)
- Social media batch content (14 posts per 2-week cycle)
- Directory management and featured listing sales
- Membership and donation platform management
- Monthly financial reconciliation and reporting
- Follow-up management via Monday.com
- Technology maintenance and updates

**1.3 Annual Conference Production** *(if applicable — attach Scope of Work)*
- Full production of Chapter's annual cannabis conference per the KYCannaCon Conference Playbook model

---

## 2. Payment — Revenue Share

**2.1 No Upfront Cost**
Chapter pays no setup fees, retainer, or management fees upfront. VMP is compensated exclusively from revenue generated through the systems it builds and manages.

**2.2 Revenue Share Structure**
VMP retains the following percentages from gross revenue collected through VMP-managed platforms:

| Revenue Stream | VMP Retains | Chapter Receives |
|---|---|---|
| Membership dues | 20% | 80% |
| Donations | 15% | 85% |
| Directory featured listings | 25% | 75% |
| Merchandise sales | 20% | 80% |
| Conference ticket sales | 15% | 85% |
| Conference vendor fees | 20% | 80% |
| Conference sponsorships | 20% | 80% |
| All other VMP-generated revenue | 20% | 80% |

**2.3 Minimum Management Fee**
Once the Chapter's total monthly revenue managed by VMP exceeds **$8,000**, VMP's monthly fee shall be the greater of:
(a) the revenue share amounts calculated under Section 2.2, or
(b) **$1,000 per month**

**2.4 Payment Collection**
VMP serves as the account manager on all Chapter payment platforms (Zeffy and equivalents). VMP collects all revenue on behalf of the Chapter, retains its share, and distributes the remainder to the Chapter. This structure ensures timely payment without requiring the Chapter to invoice or transfer funds to VMP.

**2.5 Monthly Reconciliation**
Within **5 business days** of the end of each calendar month, VMP will provide the Chapter with a written reconciliation showing:
- Total revenue collected by stream
- VMP's fee by stream
- Net amount distributed to Chapter
- Year-to-date totals

**2.6 Distribution**
VMP distributes the Chapter's net revenue to the Chapter's designated bank account within **7 business days** of month end.

---

## 3. Data and Platform Ownership

**3.1 Chapter Owns Its Data**
All subscriber lists, donor records, member records, and contact data belong to the Chapter. VMP manages this data on behalf of the Chapter and does not own it.

**3.2 Platform Accounts**
All platform accounts (Zeffy, Action Network, Google Workspace, etc.) are registered to the Chapter. VMP holds admin access for management purposes. Upon termination, VMP removes its access and full admin rights revert to the Chapter within **5 business days**.

**3.3 Content**
All content produced by VMP for the Chapter (emails, social posts, press releases, playbooks) is owned by the Chapter.

---

## 4. Term and Renewal

**4.1 Initial Term**
This Agreement begins on the date of execution and continues for **12 months**.

**4.2 Automatic Renewal**
This Agreement automatically renews for successive 12-month terms unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least **60 days** before the end of the current term.

---

## 5. Termination

**5.1 Termination for Convenience**
Either Party may terminate this Agreement with **60 days written notice**.

**5.2 Termination for Cause**
Either Party may terminate immediately if the other Party:
- Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within 14 days of written notice
- Becomes insolvent or ceases operations
- Engages in conduct that causes material harm to the other Party's reputation

**5.3 Post-Termination**
Upon termination, VMP will:
- Complete monthly reconciliation through the termination date
- Distribute all Chapter funds within 7 business days
- Transfer all platform access to the Chapter within 5 business days
- Provide an export of all Chapter data

---

## 6. Confidentiality

Each Party agrees to keep the other Party's financial information, donor lists, member data, and business operations confidential. This obligation survives termination of this Agreement.

---

## 7. Limitation of Liability

VMP's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by VMP to the Chapter in the preceding 3 months. Neither Party is liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages.
