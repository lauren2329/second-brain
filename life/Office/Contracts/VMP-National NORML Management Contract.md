## 2. Scope of Services

VMP provides full-cycle event management including:

**Pre-Event**
- Venue research, site visits, and contract negotiation
- Sponsorship package development and sales
- Vendor/exhibitor recruitment and management
- Speaker and agenda development (in collaboration with NORML)
- Registration and ticketing system setup and management
- Marketing and promotional campaign (in partnership with NORML)
- Chapter director outreach and registration drive (using NORML chapter contact list)
- All vendor and supplier coordination

**Event Production**
- On-site management for full event duration
- Venue and AV coordination
- Sponsor and vendor setup and management
- Registration and check-in management
- Speaker and session management
- Volunteer coordination
- Day-of logistics and problem resolution

**Post-Event**
- Financial reconciliation — full revenue and expense accounting
- Sponsor recap reports
- Attendee survey distribution and compilation
- Event debrief and recommendations for Year 2

---

## 3. NORML Responsibilities

NORML agrees to:
- Provide VMP with the full chapter director contact list for outreach and registration
- Designate a NORML staff liaison for agenda and content collaboration
- Review and approve event agenda, speaker selections, and marketing materials
- Provide NORML brand assets, logos, and co-branding approval
- Make decisions on NORML-specific content within [5] business days of VMP requests

---

## 4. Management Fee

**4.1 Base Fee**
NORML pays VMP a flat management fee of **$[AMOUNT]** for full event production services.

**Payment schedule:**
- 33% ($[AMOUNT]) upon execution of this Agreement
- 33% ($[AMOUNT]) [90 / 60] days before the Event date
- 34% ($[AMOUNT]) within 30 days after Event conclusion

**4.2 Expense Reimbursement**
NORML reimburses VMP for pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses incurred on behalf of the Event (venue deposits, printing, travel for site visits, etc.). VMP submits expense reports with receipts within 30 days of incurring expenses. Expenses over $[500] require prior written approval from NORML.

**4.3 Event Revenue**
All Event revenue (registration fees, vendor fees, sponsorships) is collected into a designated Event account. VMP manages this account and provides monthly reporting to NORML. Net event revenue belongs to NORML. VMP's compensation is the management fee only — VMP does not take a percentage of event revenue under this agreement.

---

## 5. Event Budget

VMP will prepare and submit a detailed Event budget within [30] days of execution. NORML must approve the budget in writing before VMP commits to any venue or vendor contracts. Material changes to the approved budget (over $[1,000]) require written approval from NORML.

---

## 6. Cancellation

**6.1 Cancellation by NORML**
If NORML cancels the Event:
- More than 90 days before the Event: NORML pays 33% of the management fee plus all non-recoverable expenses incurred
- 30–90 days before the Event: NORML pays 66% of the management fee plus all non-recoverable expenses
- Less than 30 days before the Event: NORML pays 100% of the management fee plus all non-recoverable expenses

**6.2 Cancellation by VMP**
If VMP cancels: VMP refunds all management fee payments received, minus expenses already incurred. VMP will assist NORML in finding a replacement producer.

**6.3 Force Majeure**
If the Event cannot occur due to circumstances beyond either Party's control (natural disaster, public health emergency, venue failure), the Parties will negotiate in good faith on fee adjustments.

---

## 7. Intellectual Property

All Event content, speaker materials, recordings, and branded materials belong to NORML. VMP may reference the Event in its portfolio and marketing materials with NORML's written permission.

---

## 8. Confidentiality

VMP agrees to keep NORML's chapter contact list, financial information, and organizational data confidential. VMP may not use the chapter contact list for any purpose other than this Event without NORML's written consent.

---

## 9. Independent Contractor

VMP is an independent contractor. VMP is responsible for its own taxes, insurance, and subcontractor relationships.

---

## 10. Term

This Agreement covers the [YEAR] NORML National Chapter Summit. A new agreement will be executed for each subsequent year's event.

---

## 11. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the **District of Columbia**. Disputes shall be resolved in D.C. courts or through binding arbitration, at NORML's election.
