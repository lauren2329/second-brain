## 1. Services

- Conference production support

## 2. Compensation

**2.1 Base Rate**
VMP pays Vendor at a rate of **$[X]/hour** for all services rendered, OR a flat monthly retainer of **$[X]/month** covering up to [X] hours of service.

**2.2 Project-Based Work**
For specific projects (conference production, new chapter onboarding), VMP and Vendor will agree on a flat project fee via Statement of Work before work begins.

Vendor submits invoices to VMP by the **5th of each month** for the prior month's services. VMP pays within **14 days** of receipt.

When VMP's compensation from a client is a revenue share, VMP passes through to Vendor **[X]%** of VMP's gross revenue share receipts, paid within 7 business days of VMP receiving client funds.
