## Filing Convention

Executed contracts are saved as:
`[Contract Type] — [Party Name] — [Date Executed].pdf`

Example: `VMP-Chapter Revenue Share Agreement — Colorado NORML — 2026-07-01.pdf`

Store in Google Drive: `VMP > Contracts > Executed`
