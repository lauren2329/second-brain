# Kentucky Cannabis Conference 2027 — Sales & Marketing Strategy

**Target Revenue:** $30,000-$50,000 (tickets + sponsorships)  
**Target Attendance:** 200-250  
**Key Launch Date:** September 2026

---

## Overview

Success depends on positioning KCC 2027 as the must-attend annual cannabis ecosystem event, not another conference. The Consumer Choice Awards program is the primary differentiator and marketing hook.

---

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Core Positioning

**"The Kentucky Cannabis Awards & Ecosystem Gathering"**

Not: "An educational conference about cannabis"  
**Yes:** "Where Kentucky's cannabis industry, patients, and consumers gather annually to celebrate and network"

### Key Messages

1. **Consumer Voice:** "Your vote determines the best cannabis businesses in Kentucky"
2. **Relationship-Focused:** "More networking, less room-hopping"
3. **Exclusive:** "Where industry leaders, policymakers, and innovators connect"
4. **High-Impact:** "Where consumer attention focuses on Kentucky cannabis"

---

## Target Audiences

### Primary Markets

**1. Dispensary Owners & Staff** (150-200 people)
- **Why attend:** Consumer Choice Awards, Best Dispensary category, business networking
- **Messaging:** "Your customers are voting. Be there when winners are announced."
- **Channels:** Dispensary owner groups, industry email lists, direct outreach

**2. Cannabis Consumers & Patients** (30-50 people)
- **Why attend:** Vote for favorite businesses, meet brands, patient education
- **Messaging:** "Your vote matters. Your voice shapes Kentucky's cannabis market."
- **Channels:** Patient groups, social media, email list

**3. Industry Vendors & Processors** (20-30 people)
- **Why attend:** Industry categories, partnership opportunities, market intelligence
- **Messaging:** "Connect with the entire supply chain. Get industry insights."
- **Channels:** Cultivator/processor associations, direct sales

**4. Regulators & Policymakers** (15-25 people)
- **Why attend:** Market intelligence, stakeholder engagement, legislative insights
- **Messaging:** "Stay ahead of industry trends. Engage directly with stakeholders."
- **Channels:** Direct relationship outreach, Kentucky Department of Health

**5. Healthcare Professionals** (10-20 people)
- **Why attend:** Medical cannabis education, patient perspectives
- **Messaging:** "Understand the patient experience. Connect with the medical cannabis community."
- **Channels:** Healthcare organizations, medical cannabis networks

**6. Media & Press** (10-15 people)
- **Why attend:** Awards coverage, industry news, story opportunities
- **Messaging:** "The biggest cannabis news of the year happens here."
- **Channels:** Press releases, direct journalist outreach

## Sales Strategy

### Sponsorship Sales (Target: $20,000-$40,000)

#### Sponsorship Tiers

**Platinum Sponsor** — $5,000
- Title sponsor opportunity (or category sponsor)
- Speaking slot (5 min remarks) OR large booth (10x10)
- Logo on all materials, website, social media
- 5 VIP passes
- Sponsor recognition during awards
- Naming rights to one meal (breakfast, lunch, or evening)

**Gold Sponsor** — $2,500
- Large booth (10x10) or speaking opportunity
- Logo on website, materials
- 3 VIP passes
- Sponsor recognition during awards

**Silver Sponsor** — $1,000
- Standard booth (8x8)
- Logo on website
- 2 passes
- Mention in program

**Awards Category Sponsor** — $1,500
- Sponsor one award category (Best Dispensary, Best Edible, etc.)
- Recognition during awards presentation
- Booth (8x8)
- 2 passes

**Supporting Sponsor** — $500
- Logo on website & materials
- 1 pass
- Booth (6x6)

**Voting Sponsor** — $750
- Sponsor the voting platform/process
- Major logo placement
- Speaking opportunity (2-3 min during awards)
- 2 passes

**Directory Sponsor** — $1,000
- Sponsor the voting directory
- Featured placement on voting site
- Logo on all voting materials
- 2 passes

#### Sales Process

**Timeline:**
- August 2026: Sales collateral ready, target list finalized
- September 2026: Sales outreach begins
- October-November 2026: Sponsor confirmations
- December 2026: Final sponsorship locked in

**Sales Channels:**
1. **Direct Sales** (Kentucky NORML team)

   - Dispensary chains
   - Major cannabis brands
   - Suppliers and vendors

2. **Partnership Sales** (via KCIA)
   - Industry association members

3. **Email Campaign**
   - Sponsorship prospectus email blast

4. **Industry Events**
   - Pitch at industry meetings
   - KCIA events/networking
   - One-on-one meetings with prospects

**Sales Targets:**
- 1-2 Platinum sponsors

- 5-8 Silver sponsors
