## Overview

We are building two recurring membership tiers on Zeffy:

- **Monthly:** $25/month — "KYNORML Member"
- **Annual:** $250/year — "KYNORML Member (Annual)"

Zeffy charges 0% platform fees for nonprofits. Donors/members have the option to leave Zeffy a tip — that is separate from your revenue and is Zeffy's model. You keep 100% of what members pay.

The goal is a live, tested Zeffy membership page with a public link that can be embedded on the KYNORML website and dropped into email campaigns.

---

## Step 1 — Log Into Zeffy

1. Go to [zeffy.com](https://www.zeffy.com) and log in with your KYNORML admin credentials.
2. From the dashboard, navigate to **Membership** in the left sidebar (or **Campaigns > Membership** depending on your Zeffy version).
3. If you do not see a Membership option, confirm your nonprofit status is verified in Zeffy. KYNORML is a 501(c)(4) — Zeffy supports both (c)(3) and (c)(4) organizations. If you hit a verification issue, contact Zeffy support directly and clarify you are a 501(c)(4) social welfare organization.

---

## Step 2 — Create a New Membership Campaign

1. Click **Create a membership campaign** (or **New Campaign** and select Membership).
2. **Campaign name:** `KYNORML Member`
3. Set the campaign to **active** (not draft) when ready to publish — keep as draft until all steps are complete.
4. Add the KYNORML logo and any relevant header image. Keep it clean and professional — no stock cannabis graphics.

---

## Step 3 — Set Up Two Membership Tiers

Create both tiers within the same campaign.

**Tier 1 — Monthly**
- Name: `KYNORML Member`
- Billing: Recurring
- Frequency: Monthly
- Price: **$25.00/month**
- Description (short): "Stay connected, stay informed. Cancel anytime."

**Tier 2 — Annual**
- Name: `KYNORML Member (Annual)`
- Billing: Recurring
- Frequency: Annual
- Price: **$250.00/year**
- Description (short): "Same membership, two months free. Best value."
- Note: $250/year saves $50 compared to $300/year on monthly — make this visible. Zeffy may let you add a "Best Value" badge or label; use it if available.

---

## Step 4 — Write the Membership Page Description

Use this copy on the Zeffy campaign page. Edit as needed, but keep the value propositions intact.

---

**Become a KYNORML Member**

KYNORML has been fighting for Kentucky cannabis patients for eight years. Membership is how you join the organized community behind that fight.

**As a KYNORML member, you receive:**

- **Community recognition** — your name on the KYNORML website supporter list
- **Member-only legislative updates** — direct dispatches from the Kentucky General Assembly when cannabis bills are active
- **Early access to KYCannaCon tickets** — before they go public, members get first access
- **Supporter badge** — recognition as a sustaining member of Kentucky's leading cannabis advocacy organization

Choose monthly ($25/month) or annual ($250/year — saves $50).

KYNORML is a 501(c)(4) nonprofit advocacy organization. Your membership supports ongoing cannabis advocacy and education in Kentucky. Membership fees are not tax-deductible.

---

## Step 5 — Publish the Page and Get the Link

1. Review all settings and tier details.
2. Click **Publish** (or **Save and Activate**).
3. Copy the public URL for the membership campaign page. It will look something like: `zeffy.com/membership/kynorml-member`
4. Save this link in [[KYNORML Revenue and Marketing System]] and your password manager / org reference doc. You will use this link repeatedly.

---

## Step 6 — Test It

**Do this before sending to anyone.**

1. Open the membership page in an incognito/private browser window (to simulate a real user who is not logged in).
2. Select the monthly $25 tier.
3. Complete a test signup. You can use a real card and then cancel/refund immediately, or check whether Zeffy offers a test mode (some versions do).
4. Confirm:
   - The page loads correctly
   - Both tiers display with correct pricing
   - The payment form works
   - You receive a confirmation email as the member
   - The transaction appears in your Zeffy dashboard
5. If anything breaks or looks wrong — fix it before proceeding.

---

## Step 7 — Add the Membership Link to the Website and Email Signature

Once the page is live and tested:

1. **Website homepage:** Add a "Become a Member" button or link in a visible location. Do not bury it.
2. **Email signature (Lauren's):** Add a line: `Support KYNORML — Become a Member: [link]`
3. **Website footer or navigation:** Add "Membership" as a menu item linking to the Zeffy page.

---

## Step 8 — Send the Membership Drive Email

Once Steps 1–7 are complete and verified:

1. Open [[Track 1 - Email Campaign System]].
2. Navigate to **Campaign 6 — Membership Drive**.
3. Copy the email template, insert the Zeffy membership page link.
4. Schedule or send to the full 8,000-person list.
5. Set up the non-opener follow-up automation per the Campaign 6 instructions (5-day delay, different subject line).
