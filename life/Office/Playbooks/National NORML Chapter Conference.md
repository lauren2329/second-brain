## Overview

A national conference specifically for NORML state chapter directors and leadership — built and produced by [[Verdant Management Partners]] for National NORML.

This does not currently exist. KYNORML proposes it, VMP builds it.

**Client:** National NORML
**Producer:** [[Verdant Management Partners]]
**Purpose:** Bring all state chapter directors together annually to share best practices, align on national strategy, network with vendors and sponsors, and strengthen the chapter network

**Why this matters for VMP:** This is the flagship professional services engagement — national visibility, recurring annual contract, and the credibility that makes every other chapter consulting engagement easier to sell.

---

## The Problem This Solves for National NORML

State chapters operate in silos. Most chapters are under-resourced, running on volunteer energy, with no shared playbook and no structured way to learn from each other.

The chapters that are thriving — like KYNORML — have built systems through trial and error over years. The ones that are struggling are reinventing the wheel.

A national chapter conference:
- Transfers institutional knowledge from strong chapters to developing ones
- Aligns all chapters on national campaign participation (action alerts, Smoke the Vote, press)
- Creates a pipeline: chapter directors meet vendors who can then exhibit at state conferences
- Strengthens the national movement by strengthening each state

National NORML gets a stronger chapter network. VMP gets a production contract. Every chapter gets a connection to resources they don't currently have.

## Conference Concept

**Name (suggested):** NORML National Chapter Summit
**Audience:** State chapter directors, chapter board members, chapter staff
**Size:** 50–150 attendees (one chapter = one state; some states bring 2–3 people)
**Format:** 1–2 days
**Cadence:** Annual
**Location:** Rotating by region OR fixed at a central hub city

### Agenda Pillars

1. **National NORML State of the Movement** — national leadership presents priorities, campaigns, legislative outlook
2. **Chapter Showcase** — strong chapters present what's working (KYCannaCon is a natural showcase)
3. **Working Sessions** — small group workshops on revenue, marketing, events, advocacy
4. **Vendor Expo** — cannabis industry vendors exhibit and connect with chapter directors (these become chapter conference vendors back home)
5. **Networking** — structured and unstructured time for chapter directors to build relationships with each other

## Revenue Model (for National NORML)

VMP designs and manages this. National NORML keeps the revenue.

| Stream | Notes |
|---|---|
| Chapter registration fees | Each attending chapter pays a registration fee — covers base costs |
| Individual attendee tickets | Chapter staff and guests beyond the primary director ticket |
| Vendor expo fees | Cannabis businesses pay to exhibit — connects them to 50 state chapter networks simultaneously |
| National sponsorships | Major cannabis brands, ancillary businesses — national reach at one event |
| Workshop fees | Optional paid deep-dive sessions |

**Revenue principle:** Vendor and sponsor fees should cover all production costs. Registration fees are net contribution to National NORML.

---

## VMP Revenue Model

VMP is compensated by National NORML for producing the conference.

**Pricing options to propose:**

| Model | Structure |
|---|---|
| Flat production fee | VMP charges a fixed management fee for full production — regardless of revenue outcome |
| Fee + performance | Base fee + % of net revenue above a threshold |
| Cost-plus | VMP covers all expenses and invoices National NORML at cost + management margin |

**Recommended:** Flat production fee for year one (builds trust, predictable for both parties), moving to fee + performance in year two once attendance and revenue are established.

## The Pitch to National NORML

### What VMP Brings to the Table

- **KYCannaCon** — 3 years of proven annual cannabis conference production in Kentucky
- **Chapter expertise** — 8 years operating a state NORML chapter, deep knowledge of what chapters need
- **The chapter replication system** — VMP has already built the playbook that every chapter needs; the conference is the delivery mechanism
- **National network** — already connected to national NORML through KYNORML's ongoing relationship

### What VMP Asks For

- A management contract for full conference production
- Access to National NORML's chapter director contact list for outreach
- National NORML's name, brand, and endorsement on all conference materials
- A co-planning relationship with national NORML staff for agenda and content

### What National NORML Gets

- A conference they don't have to produce themselves
- A stronger chapter network
- A new revenue stream (vendor and sponsor fees)
- Annual alignment across all 50 state chapters
- A showcase for the national movement's strength
