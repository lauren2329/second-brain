## Overview

KYNORML, INC is an 8-year-old 501(c)(4) cannabis advocacy organization in Kentucky. It has a fully built asset base — email list, conference brand, business directory, merch, memberships, and free national resources — that can be connected into a self-sustaining revenue and marketing system.

This document is also the foundation for the **NORML Chapter Replication Box** — a packaged consulting model for other state chapters.

## The Marketing Machine

Everything that drives awareness. Most of it is free.

### Always-On Channels

**National NORML Content + Graphics**
- National NORML produces education, policy updates, and graphics for all states
- KYNORML repurposes this content for Kentucky audience
- Reduces content creation burden to near zero for baseline posting

**News PR**
- When cannabis news drops nationally or in Kentucky, KYNORML responds
- Press releases, social posts, email alerts
- Positions KYNORML as the authoritative KY cannabis voice

**Email List (8k)**
- Primary direct-to-audience channel
- Used for: donation campaigns, event announcements, merch drops, member asks, matching campaigns
- All KYNORML marketing originates from written content first, then distributes to email and social

**Social Media**
- Batch content created 2 weeks at a time
- Scheduled via Buffer or Meta Business Suite (free)
- Sources: national NORML content, KYCannaCon updates, directory highlights, member spotlights, news reactions

**Directory**
- Hub for all inbound search traffic
- Patients, consumers, and businesses find KYNORML through it
- Converts visitors into email subscribers and members

### Paid Channel (Free via Grant)

**Google Ad Grants — $10,000/month**
- Runs under [[Common Good Network 501c3]]
- Targets Kentucky search traffic for cannabis resources, patient info, conference, directory
- Points to: directory, KYCannaCon registration, CGN donation page, email capture
- Requires: conversion tracking setup, active campaigns, 5% CTR maintenance
- Status: **idle — needs activation**

## Revenue Streams

### Recurring Revenue

**Memberships**
- Platform: Zeffy (0% fees)
- Current state: donation-style, no formal tiers
- Target: add 3 tiers with clear benefits
  - Community: $5/month — newsletter, member badge
  - Advocate: $25/month — above + merch discount, early event access
  - Champion: $100/month — above + directory feature, named in conference program
- Goal: 100 members at average $25/month = $2,500/month recurring

**Featured Directory Listings**
- Businesses already in directory are warm leads
- Upgrade offer: featured placement, logo, link, highlighted profile
- Price: $50–$100/month or $500–$1,000/year per listing
- Goal: 20 featured listings at $75/month = $1,500/month recurring

**Merch**
- Existing product line
- Promoted through email campaigns, social, and at KYCannaCon
- Passive with periodic campaign pushes

### Event Revenue (Annual)

**KYCannaCon — Annual Cannabis Conference**
- KYNORML's primary fundraising event
- 3 years of established brand and track record

| Revenue Line | Mechanics |
|---|---|
| Sponsorships | Tiered packages sold upfront before event date. Directory businesses are the primary prospect list. |
| Vendor tables | Cannabis businesses, ancillary services, and nonprofits pay for exhibit space |
| Ticket sales | General admission + VIP tiers. Presale to email list first. |

**KYCannaCon Sponsorship Tiers (suggested)**
- Bronze: $500 — logo in program + social mention
- Silver: $1,000 — above + table at event
- Gold: $2,500 — above + logo on stage signage + email feature
- Platinum: $5,000 — above + speaking slot + premier placement

**Presale strategy:** sponsorships and early bird tickets go on sale before the event date is finalized. Past sponsors and attendees are first contact.

### Campaign Revenue

**Donation Campaigns**
- Platform: Zeffy
- Triggered by: news events, milestones, end-of-year, matching campaigns
- Delivered via email list + social
- Standard ask: $25–$50

**Matching Gift Campaigns**
- Identify one anchor donor willing to match up to $X
- Email the 8k list: "Every dollar donated before [date] is matched"
- Doubles yield from a single campaign send

### Grant Revenue (CGN)

- Grants flow through [[Common Good Network 501c3]] (not KYNORML directly)
- CGN's educational programming — including cannabis wellness education — is grant-eligible
- Diverse earned revenue (memberships, directory fees, event revenue) strengthens grant applications by demonstrating organizational health
- See [[Greta]] for active grant tracking

## The Matching Layer

How money gets amplified across the system.

| Mechanism | How it works |
|---|---|
| Matching gift campaigns | Anchor donor matches public donations 1:1 for a set window |
| Google Ad Grants | $10k/month in free ads replaces paid marketing spend across all revenue streams |
| National NORML resources | Free content + graphics replace a marketing budget |
| Grant matching requirements | Earned revenue (listings, merch, memberships, event) proves organizational health to funders and satisfies match requirements |
