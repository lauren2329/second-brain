## Overview

The problem with social media is daily friction. This system removes it.
One 2-hour batch session every 2 weeks. 14 posts created and scheduled. Done.
National NORML provides the raw material — you provide the Kentucky lens.

Platforms: **[LIST YOUR PLATFORMS — Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter, etc.]**
Scheduling tool: **Buffer or Meta Business Suite (both free)**

---

## Content Pillars

Every post fits one of five pillars. This prevents blank-page paralysis.

| Pillar | What it is | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Patient Resources** | Directory highlights, how to access KY medical cannabis, patient tips | KYNORML directory, KY cannabis program |
| **Policy + News** | KY legislation updates, national cannabis policy changes | National NORML, KY legislature, news |
| **Education** | Cannabis wellness, science, myths vs. facts | National NORML content (free to use) |
| **Community** | KYCannaCon updates, member spotlights, event recaps, team | Internal |
| **Action** | Donation asks, membership, merch, petition, event tickets | Zeffy links, event registration |

---

## The Batch Workflow

### When
Every 2 weeks. Block 2 hours. Do not create content outside this window.

### The Session (2 hours)

**Hour 1 — Source content (30 min) + Draft posts (30 min)**

1. Go to national NORML's social media or website — pull 3–4 recent posts or articles relevant to Kentucky
2. Check KY cannabis news — any bills, program updates, patient stories from the past 2 weeks
3. Check your own calendar — any upcoming KYCannaCon news, directory highlights, events
4. Draft 14 posts using the 2-week template below

**Hour 2 — Format + Schedule (60 min)**

1. Resize graphics if needed (national NORML graphics or Canva)
2. Load all 14 posts into scheduling tool with dates and times
3. Done — do not touch social media again until next batch session

## Post Formats

**Standard post (non-graphic):**
> [1–2 sentence hook]
> [Context or fact]
> [CTA or link]
> [Hashtags: #KYCannabis #KYNORML #KentuckyNORML #MedicalCannabis #CannabisAdvocacy]

**Repurposed national NORML post:**
> [Tag @NORML or credit National NORML]
> [One sentence adding Kentucky context]
> [Link if applicable]

**Directory highlight:**
> Spotlight: [Business Name]
> [One sentence about what they offer]
> Find them in the KYNORML directory → [link]

**Action post:**
> [Direct ask — one sentence]
> [Why it matters — one sentence]
> [Link — Zeffy, event registration, merch shop]

## Reactive Posts (Outside Batch)

When cannabis news breaks in Kentucky or nationally, post within 24 hours.
See [[Track 4 - PR Response System]] for the full reactive workflow.

For social specifically:
- Draft one post immediately when news breaks
- Use the news as the hook, KYNORML's position as the body
- Link to full PR or KYNORML statement if available
- This is the only time you post outside the batch schedule

## Graphics

**National NORML graphics:** Free to use. Download from national NORML's media resources.
**Canva:** For KY-specific graphics. Keep brand consistent — use KYNORML colors and logo.
**Do not create graphics from scratch** for standard batch posts. Repurpose what exists.
