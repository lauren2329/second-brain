## Overview

KYNORML's PR strategy is reactive and news-driven.
When cannabis news breaks — locally in Kentucky or nationally — KYNORML responds as the authoritative Kentucky cannabis voice.
This system makes each response take 20 minutes, not 2 hours.

---

## News Monitoring Setup

Set these up once. They run automatically.

**Google Alerts (free — alerts.google.com):**
- `Kentucky cannabis`
- `Kentucky marijuana`
- `NORML Kentucky`
- `Kentucky medical cannabis`
- `Kentucky cannabis legislation`
- `HB 904 Kentucky` (or current bill number)
- `Kentucky cannabis dispensary`
- Delivery: Daily digest to your email

**National NORML:**
- Subscribe to national NORML's press release list
- Follow @NORML on social — their news is your source material

---

## Response Decision Framework

When news breaks, ask one question: **Does this directly affect Kentucky cannabis patients or the advocacy movement?**

| News Type | Response | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| KY legislation — vote, hearing, new bill | Press release + email to list + social | Within 24 hours |
| National federal cannabis policy change | Social post + email alert | Within 24 hours |
| KY cannabis business news (dispensary opens, closure, license) | Social post + directory update if relevant | Within 48 hours |
| National NORML statement or study | Repurpose for KY — social post | Within 48 hours |
| Negative cannabis news in KY (arrest, legal action) | Social post only unless it's a major case | Within 24 hours |
| Unrelated cannabis news with no KY angle | Skip | — |

[Date]

## Email Alert Template

Use for: major KY legislation, federal policy changes, anything the 8k list needs to know immediately.

**Subject:** `[What happened] — here's what it means for Kentucky`

**Body:**
> [One sentence: what happened]
>
> [Two to three sentences: what it means for Kentucky cannabis patients specifically]
>
> [One sentence: what KYNORML is doing about it or what patients should do]
>
> [Optional CTA: sign petition, donate, share, register for KYCannaCon]
>
> Lauren Bratcher
> Executive Director, KYNORML

Keep it under 150 words. Link to the full press release or source article.

## Response Workflow (20-minute execution)

1. **News breaks** → Google Alert or NORML notification arrives
2. **Decide** → use the framework above (respond or skip?)
3. **Draft** → use the appropriate template (press release, email, or social post)
4. **Fill in specifics** → news details, KY context, quote from quote bank
5. **Publish** → post social first (fastest), then email list, then press release to media list
6. **Log** → note in Monday.com: what news, what response, date

Total time if templates are used: 20 minutes.
