# KYCannaCon Year 4

**Kentucky's Annual Cannabis Policy & Industry Conference**
Early March 2027 | Louisville, KY

---

## Why Year 4 Is Different

This is the first KYCannaCon since Kentucky licensed dispensaries opened their doors.

KYCannaCon Year 4 takes place during the Kentucky General Assembly session — when cannabis bills are active on the floor, legislators are engaged, and press is watching. Your brand is in the room when it matters most.

**This is not an expo. This is where policy gets made.**

---

## Who Attends

Licensed operators. Dispensaries. Attorneys. Advocates. Patients. Policymakers.

- **Attendance target:** 175–200 attendees
- **KYNORML email list reach:** 8,000 subscribers
- Audience is decision-makers, not browsers

---

## Sponsorship Tiers

### Presenting Sponsor — $8,500 *(one available)*
- Premier logo placement on all event materials, signage, and digital promotion
- Named in all press releases and media outreach
- Verbal recognition at opening and closing of conference
- Full-page feature in event program
- Social media spotlight across KYNORML platforms (8,000 email subscribers + social)
- Complimentary table or display space at event
- 4 complimentary conference registrations
- "Presented by [Your Brand]" in all conference titles and communications

---

### Session Sponsor — $3,000 *(4–5 available, one per session)*
- Brand named as sponsor of a specific conference session
- Logo on session signage and presentation slides
- Recognition by session moderator from the stage
- Logo in event program alongside session description
- Social media mention tied to session topic
- 2 complimentary conference registrations

---

### Networking Reception Sponsor — $2,500 *(one available)*
- Exclusive sponsorship of the conference networking reception
- Brand recognition on all reception signage and materials
- Logo on event program reception page
- Social media feature spotlighting reception sponsorship
- 2 complimentary conference registrations

---

### Community Sponsor — $1,000
- Logo in conference program
- Recognition on KYNORML event webpage
- Social media mention
- 1 complimentary conference registration

## About KYNORML

KYNORML is Kentucky's chapter of the National Organization for the Reform of Marijuana Laws. For eight years, we have been the leading voice for cannabis patients and policy reform in the Commonwealth. KYCannaCon is our annual conference — three years running, now entering Year 4 at a pivotal moment in Kentucky cannabis history.
