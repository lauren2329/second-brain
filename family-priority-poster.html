Our Family Priority System

When life gets overwhelming — start at the bottom & work up
1 🔥 SURVIVE
Must happen — health, safety & income at stake
💊 Meds 💧 Water 🍽️ Meals 😴 Sleep 🚿 Hygiene 🧦 Clean clothes for tomorrow 🚽 Working toilet/shower 💡 Essential bills 🚶 Walk/stretch ☀️ Sunlight 🍳 Dishes for next meal 📚 Homeschool minimum
50–60% daily
2 ⚖️ STABILIZE
Prevents tomorrow's crisis — keeps systems running
🧺 Routine laundry 🍽️ Routine dishes 🗑️ Trash 📬 Mail 📋 Paperwork 🏥 Appointments 🧹 Functional spaces 📅 Scheduling 🥗 Meal planning 💰 Budget tracking 📧 Important emails 📱 Screen boundaries
20–30% daily
3 🌱 GROW
Improves the future — invest in yourself & each other
🏃 Exercise 📖 Reading 🎓 Certifications 🏫 Homeschool enrichment 💼 Business building 🛠️ New skills 🌿 Nature time 🧠 Therapy homework 🤝 Independence skills
10–20% daily
4 🎉 ENJOY
Makes life worth living — rest & play are not optional
🎮 Gaming 🎨 Art & crafts 🎵 Music 📺 Movies/TV 🏕️ Camping 🎲 Board games 📚 Library trips 🌳 Fun outings 🧶 Hobbies 🎠 Recreation
~10% daily
SURVIVE → STABILIZE → GROW → ENJOY

⏱️ Daily Time Guide

🔥 Survive: 8–10 hrs
⚖️ Stabilize: 3–5 hrs
🌱 Grow: 1–3 hrs
🎉 Enjoy: 1–2 hrs
Some days you never leave Survive. That is not failure.
That is where your energy needed to be.

🧠 The One Rule

"What is the lowest level that is currently unmet?"
No clean underwear → Work on SURVIVE 🔥
Sink overflowing → Work on STABILIZE ⚖️
Want a certification → Work on GROW 🌱
Want to play Minecraft → Work on ENJOY 🎉
SURVIVE → STABILIZE → GROW → ENJOY

Today's goal is not to reach the top.
Today's goal is to strengthen the lowest level that needs attention.
