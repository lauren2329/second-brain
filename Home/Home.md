## Structure Guide

This vault is organized into 6 main areas plus System configuration:

**Projects:** Active organizational work (Kentucky NORML, Common Good Network, etc.)  
**Business:** Operations, templates, revenue models, and business resources  
**Knowledge:** Your knowledge base (wiki for organized knowledge, raw for inputs)  
**Personal:** Life management (routines, planning, relationships, health)  
**Resources:** Templates and reference materials  
**Archive:** Completed projects and historical materials  
**System:** Configuration, MOCs, and Obsidian settings
